KABUPATEN KEDIRI

Kediri is my hometown. Kediri is located 130 km southwest of Surabaya and is the third largest city in East Java after Surabaya and Malang. The city of Kediri is divided by the Brantas River which stretches from south to north for 7 kilometers.

My house is in Kediri Regency, more precisely, Ringinrejo Regency, which is the southernmost part of Kediri. My house is quite far from the city. It took about an hour to reach downtown Kediri. In fact, I often prefer to go to Blitar City rather than my own city because my house is closer to Blitar.
